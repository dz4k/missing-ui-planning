No.

## Headless
Yes, via HeadlessMantineProvider.
> "An alternative to the standard MantineProvider is the HeadlessMantineProvider, which removes all style-related features... offering a clean slate for custom styling." (https://mantine.dev/styles/unstyled/)

## Has styles
Yes.
> "Mantine comes with more than 100 customizable components and 50 hooks to cover you in any situation." (https://mantine.dev/)

## Styleable
Config-styleable (Default) / Fully styleable (Headless).
> "Mantine components support an unstyled prop. This prop removes the library's default styles from a component, allowing it to be styled entirely from scratch." (https://mantine.dev/styles/unstyled/)
