## Framework-independent
Partial. While a design language, implementations are framework-specific (React, Angular, Vue).
> "Ant Design is fundamentally a 'design language' or 'design system' that is considered framework-agnostic." (https://dumbo.design/design-systems/ant-design/)

## Headless
No.
> "Ant Design does not operate as a 'headless UI' library... Instead, it offers a complete, opinionated design system with pre-defined styles." (https://stackshare.io/ant-design)
